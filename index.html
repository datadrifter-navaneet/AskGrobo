Identity & Security

Aadhaar-Backed
Citizen Verification

Every submission is tied to a verified citizen identity. Secure, fast, and fully UIDAI-compliant authentication in under 30 seconds.

AADHAAR VERIFICATION
Enter OTP sent to ••••••7823
OTP valid for 10 minutes. Linked to your Aadhaar number.
● Encrypted · UIDAI Compliant · Zero Data Stored
  • 1

    Enter Aadhaar Number

    Citizen enters their 12-digit Aadhaar. System initiates OTP request via UIDAI gateway with end-to-end encryption.

  • 2

    Receive & Enter OTP

    A 6-digit OTP is sent to the Aadhaar-linked mobile. Valid for 10 minutes. Auto-fills on mobile devices.

  • 3

    Identity Confirmed

    UIDAI validates the OTP. Citizen profile is created or retrieved. No Aadhaar data is stored — only a hashed reference.

  • 4

    Secure Session Begins

    A JWT-secured session is issued. All submissions under this session are legally traceable to the verified citizen.

FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

AskGrobo currently supports Tamil and English with automatic language detection. The system identifies the language used by the citizen and responds in the same language throughout the entire interaction. Support for additional Indian languages including Telugu, Kannada, and Hindi is planned for Phase 2.
Citizens enter their 12-digit Aadhaar number, which triggers an OTP to their Aadhaar-linked mobile number via the UIDAI gateway. Upon successful OTP validation, a secure session is created. AskGrobo never stores Aadhaar numbers — only a tokenized reference compliant with UIDAI guidelines.
The AI classification engine uses a fine-tuned NLP model trained on thousands of government grievance categories and department-specific terminology. It analyzes the subject, keywords, location context, and historical routing patterns to determine the correct department with over 96% accuracy. Misrouted complaints can be re-classified by department officers.
When an SLA deadline is approaching (24 hours before), an automated escalation alert is sent to the supervising officer. If the deadline is breached, the complaint is automatically escalated to the next authority level and flagged on the executive dashboard. Repeat breach patterns trigger a formal escalation report to department heads.
AskGrobo uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. The infrastructure is deployed on Indian data centers (AWS Mumbai region) for data sovereignty compliance. We follow CERT-In cybersecurity guidelines and all access is governed by role-based access control (RBAC) with full audit logs.
